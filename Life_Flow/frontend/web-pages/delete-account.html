Request Account Deletion

To delete your LifeFlow account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the LifeFlow App on your device.
  2. Go to Profile -> Edit Profile.
  3. Tap on Delete Account at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Confirm your choice.

Alternatively, you can email us at support@simats.com with the subject "Delete Account Request" and include your registered email address.

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

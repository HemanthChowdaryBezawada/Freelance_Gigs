Delete My Account

We are sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your LifeFlow account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the LifeFlow app.
  2. Go to Profile > Settings.
  3. Tap on Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your action. This is irreversible.

Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing our support team.

Please send an email with the subject "DELETE ACCOUNT REQUEST" from your registered email address.

Email Support

Data Retention

Once your account is deleted, all your personal data, habits, and tasks will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
